Brief Biography

I am a Master student at the University of Freiburg in Computer Science department supervised by Prof. Frank Hutter. The main interest of my research is practical extensions of blackbox optimization on expensive functions. More specifically, I have been tackling multi-objective optimization, constraint optimization, meta-learning of hyperparameter optimization, and the interpretation of optimizations. For more details, see my Research Experiences. My hobby is foreign language learning and mathematics such as Lebesgue-Stieltjes integral theory and functional analysis.

Prior to the Master course, I finished a Bachelor at the University of Tokyo, Systems Innovation Faculty supervised by Prof. Chen Yu in March 2020. During my Bachelor, I was engaged in the National Institute of Advanced Industrial Science and Technology (AIST) supervised by Masaki Onishi. The details of my works are available here (My works).
