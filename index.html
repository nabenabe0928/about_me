Brief Biography

I will be a Master student at the University of Freiburg, Computer Science supervised by Prof. Frank Hutter. The main interest of my research is the acceleration of blackbox optimization on expensive function. Basically, I address the problem by considering transfer learning of previous knowledge and parallel computing. For more details, see my Research Experiences. I am also interested in "Multi-agent simulation" and "Foreign Language Education".

Prior to the Master course, I finished my Bachelor at the University of Tokyo, Systems Innovation Faculty supervised by Prof. Chen Yu in March 2020. During my Bachelor course, I was engaged in the National Institute of Advanced Industrial Science and Technology (AIST) supervised by Masaki Onishi. The details of my works are available here (Softwares).
